Use Cases

A curriculum vitae (CV) is a comprehensive record of education, experience, publications, certifications, and awards. A resume is a concise summary—typically one page—tailored to the job you are applying for.

Resumes should be targeted to each application and formatted with ATS (Applicant Tracking System) considerations in mind so recruiter tools can correctly parse and match your profile. Use clear headings, relevant keywords, and measurable outcomes.

Tips

Key tips:

  • Keep most resumes under 1,000 words; some tools truncate after ~1,000 words.
  • For technical roles (DevOps): use reverse-chronological format, highlight technologies, platforms, and measurable outcomes.
  • For functional roles (Instructor, Trainer, Scrum/Agile facilitator): emphasize skills, certifications, and facilitation experience.
  • Use keywords from the job description and quantify results where possible (percentages, time savings, team sizes).
